About Me

I am a third year computer science student at North Carolina A&T State University from Durham, North Carolina. I've had the privilege to work with my local government Durham County IS&T Department for three summers, creating multiple software applications for each county department (EMS, Social Services, Police Department, etc.) Outside of being a coder, I'm a musician with experience of 9 years of playing the piano and 1 year of playing the trombone.

Before coming to college, I originally wanted to major and focus in business management. This focus started after becoming president of Future Business Leaders of America attending networking conferences, and business programs (Financial Futures Youth Institute) that focused on marketing, management, and teamwork. Soon after, I came across the field of computer science.

Goals

Upon receiving my Bachelor's Degree, I would like to further my education by receiving my master's in computer science with a focus in health informatics & security. I would also like to build on my background by gaining more experiences with companies in the tech industry. After accomplishing these goals I hope to one day start my own tech company focusing health care tech innovation - identifying problems that the business face today and foster those problems through the development and innovation of technology in creating service-learning and assistant technology.
